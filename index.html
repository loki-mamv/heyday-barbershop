The Shop

GOOD CUTS. GOOD PEOPLE.

Heyday is the kind of barbershop Seminole Heights deserves. No appointment apps with 47 upsells, no attitude, no muzak. Just two skilled Dereks who love what they do and take it seriously enough to keep you coming back.

You'll find us on N Florida Ave doing the thing barbers have always done best — making people look and feel like themselves, only sharper. Whether you're a regular or just found us, you're welcome here.

Book online or walk in. Either way, you're leaving looking right.
